Polish Meat and Potatoes

Description

Polish styled meat and potatoes recipe from Christopher Short's grandmother.


Ingredients

  • 4 potatoes, peeled and cut into 1 inch cubes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 green bell peppers, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 (16 ounce) package kielbasa sausage, cut into 1 inch pieces

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook onions and potatoes for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Reduce flame to med and stir in bell pepper, onion powder, garlic powder, salt and pepper.
  4. Cover and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in kielbasa, cover, and cook for 15 minutes, or until onions are caramelized.
